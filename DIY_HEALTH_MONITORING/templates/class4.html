Immediate Medical Attention Required

EMERGENCY MEDICAL ATTENTION REQUIRED

Your health parameters indicate a critical condition that requires immediate medical evaluation. Do not delay seeking professional medical care. This could indicate a serious underlying health issue that needs urgent treatment.

CALL EMERGENCY (108) EMERGENCY MEDICAL (102)

Go to Nearest Hospital

Proceed immediately to the nearest emergency room or hospital. Do not drive yourself - call an ambulance or have someone drive you.

Inform Medical Staff

Tell emergency staff about your symptoms, when they started, and share your health monitoring results. Mention any pre-existing conditions.

Bring Medications

Take all current medications with you to the hospital. Include prescription drugs, over-the-counter medicines, and supplements.

Critical Condition Dietary Management

Avoid Harmful Fats
Eliminate saturated and trans fats. Avoid fried foods, processed meats, and high-fat dairy products immediately.
Increase Fiber Intake
Focus on soluble fiber from oats, apples, and legumes to help manage cholesterol and blood sugar levels.
Fresh Fruits & Vegetables
Emphasize antioxidant-rich fruits and vegetables. Berries, leafy greens, and citrus fruits provide essential nutrients.
Limit Alcohol & Caffeine
Completely avoid alcohol and limit caffeine intake. These can exacerbate cardiovascular and other critical health issues.

⚠️ IMMEDIATE DIETARY RESTRICTIONS

  • Avoid all processed and canned foods high in sodium
  • Eliminate sugary beverages and refined carbohydrates
  • Stop consumption of red meat and high-fat proteins
  • Avoid adding salt to foods during preparation or at table
  • Limit portion sizes and eat smaller, more frequent meals

Emergency Lifestyle Modifications

Weight Management

Work with healthcare providers to develop a safe weight management plan. Even 5-10% weight loss can significantly improve critical health markers.

Smoking Cessation

Immediately stop all tobacco use. Seek medical support for smoking cessation programs and nicotine replacement therapies.

Stress Reduction

Implement immediate stress management techniques: deep breathing, meditation, and avoid stressful situations until medically stabilized.
